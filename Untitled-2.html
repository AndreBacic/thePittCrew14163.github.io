About Us 

About

We’re The Pitt Crew, a robotics team made up of six homeschooled students from North Carolina. Our team participates in the annual FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC). This is our third year in the league and we're learning more every year!

Through hands on robotics, we have learned valuable skills including  engineering, programing, leadership and many more! Through FIRST® we have gone from not understanding how to make robot move to qualifiying to the state championship, and learned a LOT in the process.
