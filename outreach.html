Out reach

The Pitt Crew has told many people about FIRST. We went to a local school and showed our robot
to the kids who were inspired to participate in FIRST. We demonstrated our robot at a fair where
500+ people possily saw our robot. We held a yard sale where we raised money for our team and
told people about FIRST. And those are just a few of the places we've told people about FIRST!

The Pitt Crew Participates in The First Tech Chalenge,
a robotics competion for students from 7th to 12th grade.
